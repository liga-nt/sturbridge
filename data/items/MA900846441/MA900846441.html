Item: MA900846441

Two friends bought a chocolate bar.

 

  • One friend ate 28 of the chocolate bar.
  • The other friend ate 18 of the chocolate bar.

 

Create a fraction model in which the shaded part represents the fraction of the chocolate bar that remains.

 

Divide the figure into the correct number of equal parts by using the More and Fewer buttons. Then shade by selecting the part or parts.
